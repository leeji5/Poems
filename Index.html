No Matter What
By Rosalee Sarmiento

I love you no matter what you do,
No matter what they say of you.
No matter how long I have to wait,
Even if love feels like fate turned late.

Even if you don't want me near,
Even if you choose to push me away.
I still love you, though you never choose me,
Always someone else.

Even if you change, or everything fades,
My love stays firm, through shifting shades.
I’ll keep holding on, through every doubt,
Even when your world shuts me out.

I long to know your storm and fire,
To understand your heart’s desire.
Though it feels impossible to break through,
I still want to trust what’s left of you.

I regret the day I let you go,
A quiet ache I never show.
It's torture, yes this hollow space,
This aching pull I can’t replace.

Something's missing, something's gone,
But still, my love goes on and on.
So here I stay, through push and shove,
With nothing left but endless love.

My First Flower
By Rosalee Sarmiento

I received my first flower
A simple one, not real,
But still,
The most special gift I’ve ever held.

It wore my favorite color,
A warm, simple brown flower.
I’ve never liked flowers
Too fragile, too useless,
But this one felt different.

I’m in love,
Obsessed, even
Was it the beauty of the flower,
Or the person who gave it to me?

When I gaze at it,
I eventually smile.
It’s as if heaven peeked through the petals,
And for a moment,
I saw a beauty that took me by surprise.

I Think I Did Everything I Could
By Rosalee Sarmiento

I think I did everything I could,
Stood where no one else ever would.
Fought the storms with open hands,
Built you castles out of sand.

I waited, hoping you'd understand,
Turned the tide, rewrote the plans.
Gave you my heart, let go of my pride,
Made the impossible possible, just to stay by your side.

But still, you chose someone else,
Someone who won’t bleed for you like I do.
Someone who’ll never climb your walls,
Or set sail through your drowning thoughts.

I don’t know what I lacked or missed,
When all I ever wanted was for you to love me.
What more could I have ever done,
When I gave you everything I have?
